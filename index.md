## Overview
This index maps authoritative continuing education resources to the 2018 MAGIRT Core Competencies. 

## How to use this Index
Select a domain above to view the specific competencies and curated resources.

### Priority Levels
Following the research of **Bishop & Larsen (2024)**, we have labeled competencies with a mean importance score of **3.5 or higher** as **[Priority: High]**. These areas represent the most critical service-oriented and foundational skills identified by current practitioners.
