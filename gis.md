## II.B. Resource Management
#### II.B.1. Data Discovery and Acquisition **[Priority: High]** <small>*(Score: 3.59)*</small>
* Knowledge of spatial data resources
    * [UCGIS BoK: Data Sources](https://gistbok.ucgis.org/bok-topics/data-sources)
    * [UCGIS BoK: Open Data](https://gistbok.ucgis.org/bok-topics/open-data)
* Knowledge of copyright limitations, licensing issues and acquisition details
    * [UCGIS BoK: Intellectual Property Rights](https://gistbok.ucgis.org/bok-topics/intellectual-property-rights)
* Knowledge of datums, projections, and coordinate systems
    * [UCGIS BoK: Coordinate Systems](https://gistbok.ucgis.org/bok-topics/coordinate-systems)
    * [UCGIS BoK: Map Projections](https://gistbok.ucgis.org/bok-topics/map-projections)
* Knowledge of spatial metadata standards and issues
    * [UCGIS BoK: Metadata](https://gistbok.ucgis.org/bok-topics/metadata)
* Competence in building spatial data discovery tools
    * [UCGIS BoK: Search and Discovery](https://gistbok.ucgis.org/bok-topics/search-and-discovery)

#### II.B.2. Data Creation and Transformation <small>*(Score: 3.24)*</small>
* Knowledge of map digitization and georeferencing processes
    * [UCGIS BoK: Digitizing](https://gistbok.ucgis.org/bok-topics/digitizing)
    * [UCGIS BoK: Georeferencing](https://gistbok.ucgis.org/bok-topics/georeferencing)
* Knowledge and ability to transform acquired geospatial data
    * [UCGIS BoK: Geometric Transformations](https://gistbok.ucgis.org/bok-topics/geometric-transformations)
* Ability to perform advanced spatial and geoprocessing activities

#### II.B.3. Data Management **[Priority: High]** <small>*(Score: 3.59)*</small>
* Ability to develop and organize a GIS data collection
    * [UCGIS BoK: Data Management Systems](https://gistbok.ucgis.org/bok-topics/data-management-systems)
* Ability to assess a GIS data collection
* Ability to create a data portal
* Understanding of digital preservation methods and techniques
    * [UCGIS BoK: Digital Preservation](https://gistbok.ucgis.org/bok-topics/digital-preservation)

#### II.B.4. Information Technology <small>*(Score: 2.79)*</small>
* Current knowledge of system requirements for GIS
    * [UCGIS BoK: Computing Platforms](https://gistbok.ucgis.org/bok-topics/computing-platforms)

#### II.B.5. Technological Infrastructure and Facilities <small>*(Score: 2.74)*</small>
* Management
    * [UCGIS BoK: GIS&T System Management](https://gistbok.ucgis.org/bok-topics/gist-system-management)
* Hardware
* Software
* Policy

#### II.B.6. Online Geospatial Technologies <small>*(Score: 3.24)*</small>
* Understanding of OGC compliant geospatial web services
    * [UCGIS BoK: Web GIS](https://gistbok.ucgis.org/bok-topics/web-gis)
* Ability to teach people how to use cloud-based GIS tools
    * [UCGIS BoK: Cloud Computing](https://gistbok.ucgis.org/bok-topics/cloud-computing)
* Ability to manage organizational subscriptions and licensing
* Knowledge of GIS server technology

#### II.B.7. Related Technologies <small>*(Score: 2.32)*</small>
* Operation of large format scanners and printers
* Ability to organize data in tabular format
* Knowledge of GPS (global positioning systems) technologies
    * [UCGIS BoK: GPS and GNSS](https://gistbok.ucgis.org/bok-topics/global-navigation-satellite-systems-gnss)
* Knowledge of statistics & data visualization
    * [UCGIS BoK: Statistical Mapping](https://gistbok.ucgis.org/bok-topics/statistical-mapping)
* Knowledge of computer programming
    * [UCGIS BoK: Programming and Scripting](https://gistbok.ucgis.org/bok-topics/programming-and-scripting)
* Ability to create scripts for batch processing
